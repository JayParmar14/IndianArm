Chief of the Army Staff

Lieutenant General Devraj Anbu, PVSM, UYSM, AVSM, YSM, SM has taken over as Vice Chief of Army Staff today. Before taking over as Vice Chief of Army Staff, the General Officer was commanding Northern Command. He was commissioned into 14 Sikh Light Infantry in 1980. He has a vast experience and has served in all types of operational environment to include Siachen Glacier, counter-insurgency & counter terrorist operations in Kashmir and North East as well as Operation Pawan in Sri Lanka. He has commanded his unit during Operation Parakram, a Mountain Brigade on the Line Of Control, Mountain Division in Sikkim, Indian Military Training Team in Bhutan, and Gajraj Corps in the Eastern Theatre. He has also held a number of important staff as well as instructional appointments during his career.
